What is VFX

VFX is the art of combining reality with intention. Through compositing, tracking, simulation, and digital construction, we extend what a camera can capture. Sets become infinite. Products become cinematic. Ideas become visible.

Where it applies

Film, advertising, events, education, architecture, logistics, product launches — any industry that needs to explain, persuade, or impress can leverage VFX. If it can be imagined, it can be visualized.
